MAKERS
at USC
We are the tinkerers of USC.

Makers is USC's premier electrical engineering organization. Our members work together in teams to propose, plan, and build various projects each semester, from wearables to logic circuits. We provide our members with the knowledge, resources, and connections to create these gadgets. Through our regular workshops, company seminars, and networking events, members have an opportunity to develop technical skills and make valuable professional connections. We also foster a tight-knit community of engineers by hosting fun retreats and other social events.

Sponsored by CCI, the USC Viterbi Center for Cyber-Physical Systems and the Internet of Things
