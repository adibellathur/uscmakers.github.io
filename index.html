MAKERS
at USC
We are the tinkerers of USC.
Makers is USC's premier electrical engineering organization. From wearables to logic circuits, we provide our members with the knowledge, materials and connections to make amazing things.

Our members work in teams to create diverse, interesting projects each semester. We offer weekly meetings, regular workshops on skills such as Arduino and Internet of Things, company seminars, networking opportunities, fun retreats, and other social events!
